ORGANIZATIONAL OVERVIEW 
Green Ahom Federation (GAF) is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization established with the 
objective of creating sustainable social development among marginalized and underprivileged communities 
across Assam, particularly in the Barak Valley region comprising Cachar, Hailakandi, and Karimganj 
districts. The organization has consistently worked in areas such as disaster management, education, skill 
development, health awareness, environmental protection, women empowerment, child rights, food security, 
livelihood development, and humanitarian relief services.  
The financial year 2022â€“23 represented an important phase of expansion and grassroots intervention for 
Green Ahom Federation. During the year, the organization intensified its field-based activities focusing on 
vulnerable populations affected by poverty, flood disasters, unemployment, environmental degradation, food 
insecurity, and lack of awareness regarding social and legal rights. 
The organization adopted a participatory development approach, emphasizing direct community 
involvement, local volunteer engagement, and collaboration with educational institutions, social workers, 
and community leaders to ensure sustainable impact.
